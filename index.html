FAQ

What is NACOS ↓

NACOS stands for the National Association of Computing Students. It functions as the official umbrella organization and representative body for all students pursuing degrees in computer science and related academic fields. Membership in NACOS is typically inclusive of all individuals within these specific disciplines.

why is it so important to be a NACOS member ↓

It is highly important for students studying computer science to join this association. Beyond automatic eligibility based on your field of study, becoming a member grants you access to numerous exclusive opportunities and benefits provided by the association.

When was NACOS founded ↓

The National Association of Computing Students was founded in July 1993 as the Nigeria Assosiation of Computer Science Students(NACOSS) later changing its name and scope to NACOS in 2020 .
